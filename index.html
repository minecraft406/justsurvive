COMMANDS

Useful commands on our server

SETHOME

The Sethome command is very useful for finding your home! Whether you have found a far off revine, or have just made a home, Sethome will help you! It's simple, just do /sethome. That will set your home. Then do /home to teleport to it. The command was made from the plugin Set Home by _ForgeUser10809020! See it here on Bukkit or here on Spigot.

SHOP

The shop command makes it easy for you to sell your ores and earn money! With your money, you can buy buy almost anything a you would ever need! This involves three easy commands. /sell is to sell ores. Just place the ores you want to sell into the container, and click the ESC key! The money will be added into your account. To check how much money you have, do /money or /balance. Finally, do /shop or /buy to buy almost any item! Click on the category you want, and buy the item you want! Press ESC to go back in the menu. These commands were made from GUIShop! Visit it here on Bukkit or here on Spigot.
